###  eli

that is, then, sometimes also or, co-ordinating conjunction, "kissa eli eläin", "kissa eli koira"
* UPOS:  CCONJ
* Added from:  unihu


## eli (alternate reading 2 )

so, discourse particle, to be mumbled turn-initially or pausing, "eli eli, mitäs sit"
* UPOS:  INTJ
* Added from:  omorfi
