### ` PROPN_ACRO_OO `

* _Acronyms that end in 'h', 'k' or 'o' that is pronounced as a letter_
* Universal POS is PROPN and legacy POS is ACRONYM
* suffixes use back vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 0
* The lemmas must end in * None * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [HKOÅhkoå] `
