### ` PROPN_ACRO_AA `

* _acronyms ending in 'a' pronounced as a letter_
* Universal POS is PROPN and legacy POS is ACRONYM
* suffixes use back vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 0
* The lemmas must end in * None * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [Aa] `
