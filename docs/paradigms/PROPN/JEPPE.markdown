### ` PROPN_JEPPE `

* _pe stem, ppe: pe variations_
* Universal POS is PROPN and legacy POS is NOUN
* suffixes use front vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 8-B
* The lemmas must end in * pe * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [ÄÖYIEĒÉÍäöyieíüýéőèěęøæõ][^aou]*ppe `
