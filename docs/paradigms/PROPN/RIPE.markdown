### ` PROPN_RIPE `

* _pe stem, pe : ppee variations_
* Universal POS is PROPN and legacy POS is NOUN
* suffixes use front vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 48-B
* The lemmas must end in * e * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [ÄÖYIEĒÉÍäöyieíüýéőèěęøæõ][^aou]*pe `
