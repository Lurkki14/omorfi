### ` PROPN_ACRO_YY `

* _Acronyms that ends in 'y' pronounced as letter_
* Universal POS is PROPN and legacy POS is ACRONYM
* suffixes use back vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 0
* The lemmas must end in * None * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [YÜyü] `
