### ` PROPN_ACRO_ÖÖ `

* _Acronyms that end in 'ö' pronounced as letter_
* Universal POS is PROPN and legacy POS is ACRONYM
* suffixes use front vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 0
* The lemmas must end in * None * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [Öö] `
