### ` PRON_KUKIN `

* _‘kukin’ inflects before kin clitic, only ku stem_
* Universal POS is PRON and legacy POS is PRONOUN
* suffixes use back vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 101
* The lemmas must end in * ukin * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` kukin `
