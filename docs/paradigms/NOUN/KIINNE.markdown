### ` NOUN_KIINNE `

* _nne stem, nne : ntee variations front harmony_
* Universal POS is NOUN and legacy POS is NOUN
* suffixes use front vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 48-J
* The lemmas must end in * ne * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [ÄÖYIEĒÉÍäöyieíüýéőèěęøæõ][^aou]*nne `
