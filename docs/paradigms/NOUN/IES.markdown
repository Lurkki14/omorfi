* Universal POS is NOUN and legacy POS is NOUN
* suffixes use front vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 41-D
* The lemmas must end in * es * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [ÄÖYIEĒÉÍäöyieíüýéőèěęøæõ][^aou]*es `
