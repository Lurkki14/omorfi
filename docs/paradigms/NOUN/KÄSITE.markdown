### ` NOUN_KÄSITE `

* _te stem, te : ttee variations_
* Universal POS is NOUN and legacy POS is NOUN
* suffixes use front vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 48-C
* The lemmas must end in * e * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [ÄÖYIEĒÉÍäöyieíüýéőèěęøæõ][^aou]*e `
