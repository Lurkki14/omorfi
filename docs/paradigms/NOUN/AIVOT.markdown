### ` NOUN_AIVOT `

* _Plurale tantums, ot stem that inflect without variation (o stem)_
* Universal POS is NOUN and legacy POS is NOUN
* suffixes use back vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 1
* The lemmas must end in * t * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` ot `
* This is a [plurale tantum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plurale_tantum) paradigm for plural only nominals.
