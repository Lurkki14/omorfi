### ` NOUN_KIDE `

* _de stem, de : tee variations_
* Universal POS is NOUN and legacy POS is NOUN
* suffixes use front vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 48-F
* The lemmas must end in * de * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` [ÄÖYIEĒÉÍäöyieíüýéőèěęøæõ][^aou]*de `
