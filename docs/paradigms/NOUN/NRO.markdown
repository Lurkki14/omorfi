### ` NOUN_NRO `

* _Abbreviated nouns without final full stop, no inflection. At the moment covers all nominal parts-of-speech, may be split in the future_
* Universal POS is NOUN and legacy POS is PARTICLE
* suffixes use back vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 99
* The lemmas must end in * None * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` . `
