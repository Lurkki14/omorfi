### ` NUM_KUUSI `

* _si stem, si : te variations back harmony_
* Universal POS is NUM and legacy POS is NUMERAL
* suffixes use back vowel harmony
* KOTUS paradigm used in their dictionary is 27
* The lemmas must end in * si * (which will be deleted to form an invariant stub) and the regex matching the lemma is ` kuusi `
